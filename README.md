## 👋 About Me

I am an **Artificial Intelligence engineer, computer vision researcher, and software developer** with a strong interest in building practical, research-driven systems for real-world deployment. My work focuses on the intersection of **deep learning, computer vision, embedded systems, IoT, LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation, and industrial AI solutions**.

I enjoy transforming ideas into working systems: from model design and experimental validation to software implementation, deployment, documentation, and reproducible engineering workflows. My long-term goal is to contribute to intelligent systems that are **accurate, efficient, explainable, scalable, and useful in real operational environments**.
